Director’s Message: On Purpose & Pedagogy

Dear Aspirants,

At Abhipedia | Abhimanu IAS, our mission has always been to prepare not just candidates for an exam, but leaders for the nation. This new initiative at Fort Ramshehar is a natural extension of that vision—a place where discipline meets inspiration, and ancient heritage nurtures modern minds.

The UPSC examination demands more than academic knowledge. It requires clarity of thought, moral courage, and leadership ability. Through this residential programme, we aim to offer students not just expert-led academic coaching, but a life-transforming ecosystem.

Here, learning doesn’t stop at the classroom. It continues through discussion circles, AI labs, ethical debates, and leadership workshops—all within the calming walls of a historic fort.

This is your opportunity to not just prepare for a career, but to step into your larger calling. We look forward to shaping the future with you.

Warm regards,

Parveen Bansal
Director and Founder, Abhimanu IAS
